Before you begin, make sure you have:

1. The **UC Davis Harvest** application installed on your iPhone
2. Your **Kerberos login credentials**
3. A **Worker** or higher role on Harvest

---

## Logging In

1. Open the Harvest app after installation to access the login screen.
2. Tap the green **“Login with UC Davis”** button.
3. You will be redirected to **CAS Authentication**. Enter your Kerberos credentials.
4. After authenticating, you will be taken to the Harvest website. Tap **“Authorize Mobile App”**.
5. You will be redirected back to the app’s homepage. You are now logged in.

![Login flow](/harvest/harvest-mobile-login.png)

---

## Logging Out

1. Navigate to the **Settings** tab on the right side of the bottom navigation bar.
2. Tap **“Log out of Harvest”**.

![Logout flow](/harvest/harvest-mobile-logout.png)
