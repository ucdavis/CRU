Before you begin, ensure you have:

1. The **UC Davis Harvest** app installed on your iPhone
2. A **Worker** (or higher) role on Harvest
3. Access to the QR code you intend to scan

---

## Using a QR Code to Find a Project

1. Navigate to either the **Recent Projects** or **All Projects** tab.
2. Tap the **QR code icon** in the top-right corner of the screen.
3. Grant the application permission to use your camera.
4. Aim your camera at the project QR code to scan it.

![QR project](/harvest/qr1.png)

---

## Using a QR Code to Find an Expense Rate

1. Navigate to the [expense list](/documentation/harvest/mobile-add-expense).
2. Tap the **QR code icon** in the top-right corner of the expense list to open the camera.
3. Grant the application permission to use your camera.
4. Aim your camera at the expense QR code to scan it.

![QR expense](/harvest/qr2.png)
